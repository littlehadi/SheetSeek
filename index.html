WHAT WE DO

Our Beliefs


"When you’re about to begin your semester, we understand that buying your books can be a source of stress. In fact, the first few days are quite stressful, mostly for first-year students. That’s why, at SheetSeek, we believe that our product can make life easier for undergraduates by creating a platform where you can buy old textbooks from other students. Not only you will be organized, you will also make contacts in your program by using our message interface. Furthermore, we believe that with our idea, you will not have to wait in line for hours at your University’s library. With a product that was made by students and for students, we think that buying textbooks will be more affordable and will be a much easier task to do. "



Who we are


"SheetSeek was built with the main purpose of helping and facilitating students lives. We understand the amount of stress that some of you might be going through and want to create a simple platform that not only helps students, but relieves some of the responsibilities that comes along with purchasing school related products.
