OUR MISSION

SheetSeek was built with the main purpose of helping and facilitating students lives. A survey conducted in 2016 proved that more than 90 percent of students found post-secondary education to be a very stressful experience. That's why there is SheetSeek. We wanted to create a simple and helpful platform that relieves some of the responsibilities and stress that comes along with purchasing school related products.

MADE BY STUDENTS
FOR STUDENTS

FEATURES

No More Confusion, No More Stress

Buying textbooks and other school equipment is always a pain in the rear. "Which book do I need? What edition is acceptable? " Sheetseek remedies the pain by creating a list of items required for each class you have.

All Your Needs In One Place

Based off your classes, SheetSeek manages to display a list of the exact supplies and books you would need, also providing suggestions for locations to buy them and recommendations for other course related things. You'll be able to find old tests, notes, assignments and even lab gear! All of that and SheetSeek even provides discounts!

Your Own Student Community

SheetSeek serves as a centralized hub for fellow students who are trying to buy and resell their textbooks as well as other school materials in special groupchats based on course code! It enables fast and easy communication between fellow students, eliminating the burden of buying and selling.
